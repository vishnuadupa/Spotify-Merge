Setup
Step 1 · Spotify app credentials

Create a free app at developer.spotify.com/dashboard, then add this exact Redirect URI to it: http://127.0.0.1:5174/callback

Step 2 · Connect your account(s)

Connect one account to manage your own library, or two accounts to migrate between them.
